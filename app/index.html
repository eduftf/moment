How it works

Up and running in three steps

Moment runs inside Zoom as a sidebar app. A lightweight companion on your computer handles the screenshots.

1

Add to Zoom

Install Moment from the Zoom App Marketplace. It appears as a sidebar panel during your meetings.

2

Download Companion

A small helper app runs on your computer to capture screenshots. Download once, it auto-starts on login.

3

Moments captured

Screenshots are taken automatically on reactions, participant peaks, or manual trigger. All saved locally to your machine.

Features

Smart triggers, beautiful archive

Multiple ways to capture the right moment, all organized into a meeting archive you can revisit.

👏

Reaction capture

Any emoji reaction in the meeting triggers a screenshot. Choose which reactions to track in settings.

📈

Peak participants

Automatically captures when participant count hits a new high — perfect for recording full attendance.

📷

Manual capture

One tap on the Capture button takes an instant screenshot. Quick, simple, always available.

📚

Meeting archive

Every meeting gets its own folder with all screenshots, participant data, and a browsable gallery.
