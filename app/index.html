Features

Smart triggers,
local archive

01

Reaction capture

Any emoji reaction triggers a screenshot. Clap, heart, party — your choice. 5-second debounce keeps it sane.

02

Peak detection

Automatically captures when participant count hits a new maximum. Records full attendance without you lifting a finger.

03

Manual capture

One click on the sidebar button. Instant screenshot. Always available when you need it.

04

Meeting archive

Every meeting gets its own folder organized by topic. Screenshots with timestamps, ready to revisit anytime.
